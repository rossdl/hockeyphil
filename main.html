Other Information You Should Know

All sessions are held at Plymouth Ice Center. Sessions are Thursday evenings starting on May 24th and we generally start at 6:45 or 7:15 PM on most evenings.

You should check the schedule regularly to confirm start times. Although we have a published schedule those times change occasionally.

All skaters should plan on wearing protective gear with helmets, shin pads and gloves being mandatory! Mouth guards and elbow pads are highly encouraged. You will be playing at your own risk and we are not liable for injuries.

Locker rooms will not be locked during our ice time. Do not leave valuables lying around, as we are not responsible for lost or stolen articles. If you have articles of value, you should bring them with you to the ice and leave them on the player's bench.

Each skater is responsible to bring a light and dark jersey (try to avoid light greys, yellow, or reds please!) I will generally go around and assign (or suggest) certain players to certain sides but this is not always going to be the case. Use common sense please.
